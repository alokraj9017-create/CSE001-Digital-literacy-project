# 📱 Social Media Safety & Professionalism Checklist
**Student Digital Ambassador Program | Task 4**

Maintaining a positive digital footprint is essential for career growth. Use this checklist to ensure your social media presence remains an asset.

---

### ✅ The "Do's" (Best Practices)
1.  **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Always add an extra layer of security to your accounts to prevent unauthorized access.
2.  **Audit Your Privacy Settings:** Periodically check who can see your posts and personal information on platforms like Instagram and Facebook.
3.  **Think Before You Post:** Ask yourself: "Would I be comfortable if a future recruiter or professor saw this content?"
4.  **Network Professionally:** Use platforms like LinkedIn to follow industry leaders and join professional groups related to your field.
5.  **Verify Information:** Double-check the source of news or links before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation or "fake news."

---

### ❌ The "Don'ts" (Common Pitfalls)
1.  **Don't Overshare Personal Data:** Avoid posting your home address, phone number, Aadhaar details, or real-time travel locations.
2.  **Don't Engage in Cyberbullying:** Never use offensive language, post hateful comments, or participate in "troll" threads.
3.  **Don't Post While Emotional:** Avoid "venting" or posting aggressive content when you are angry; digital footprints are permanent.
4.  **Don't Plagiarize:** Never post photos, artwork, or written content created by others without giving proper credit to the original creator.
5.  **Don't Tag Others Without Consent:** Respect the privacy of your peers by asking permission before posting or tagging them in photos.

---

### 📝 Reflection Summary
A professional digital identity is built through consistent, respectful online behavior. By following these guidelines, I ensure that my social media presence reflects the values of a responsible Student Digital Ambassador.
